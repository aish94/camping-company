Digital Nomad Expedition


Camping Co’s co-working/co-living space is located inside a beautiful Tea Estate of Assam. At the center of this 2,600 Acre Tea estate is the campsite and a Colonial Bungalow surrounded by miles of Tea plantations offering a collaborative and relaxing coworking environment. The experience offers a rare combination of camping, co-working and co-living. The space is equipped with everything required for a comfortable retreat with nature. You will find yourself in a community of like-minded individuals, exchanging skills and knowledge in the pursuit of better quality of life through work-life balance.

Stay
  • Total 8 to 10 Dome Tents, all on ground, all single occupancy (two on the rooftop at extra cost).
  • Sleeping bags and sleeping mats
  • At the time of the camp being shifted, packing and unpacking of sleeping bags will be the individual's own responsibility for hygiene and to save time.
  • Mandatory registration on Aarogya Setu App
Program Details

The entire program is of 28 days. There are two legs of this excursion - 14 Days in Assam and next 14 days in Meghalaya. Depending on the leg you choose an expedition outline will be given which will involve the activities planned at that destination.You will visit the best locations of Assam and Meghalaya during the journey showcasing the countryside of Northeast India, the circuit has been designed to keep your comfort and adventure needs in mind.

Base Camp in Assam - Sapoi Tea Estate

Base Camp in Meghalaya - Camping Co. Nongkhnum Island

Expedition Inclusions:
  • All meals at the site including a mix of cooking off-the-grid
  • We will provide you with our overlanding vehicles for a round trip starting and ending in Guwahati
  • Tents, Sleeping bags, and camping gears
  • Nature Activities such as kayaking, mountain climbing, trekking, cliff diving, bonfire building, setting up camps, river excursion, yoga etc
  • Knowledge expeditions such as tea plantation overview, working of cooperative societies, in-house story sessions, nature conservation, rural governance, introduction to tribal culture etc.
  • Fuel & toll taxes
  • All Inner line permit for restricted areas
  • Local guides, assistance
  • Campsite charges
  • Airport transfers
  • Day excursions to places such as Kaziranga National Park, Nameri in Assam and Cherrapunji, Shillong in Meghalaya depending on the program outline of the particular state.
Expedition Exclusions:
  • Flight to the destination
  • Meals apart from the mentioned ones or at a restaurant
  • Drinks and other snacks
  • Any activity apart from the mentioned ones
  • Any cost arising out of unforeseen circumstances like ill-health, car breakdown or repairs, damage accruing from land blocks, bad weather etc.
  • Any Medical expenses incurred during the trip or travel insurance
  • Any charges for carrying a video camera or still camera.
